ELON MUSK

Elon Reeve Musk FRS is a business magnate and investor.

He holds South African, Canadian, and U.S. citizenship and is the founder, CEO, and lead designer of SpaceX, co-founder, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc, co-founder and CEO of Neuralink; and co-founder of PayPal.

In December 2016, he was ranked 21st on the Forbes list of The World's Most Powerful People.As of October 2018, he has a net worth of $19.8 billion and is listed by Forbes as the 46th-richest person in the world.
